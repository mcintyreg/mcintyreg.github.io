The Behemoth!

This game is all about the vehicle combat. You get an advantage if you take the raider with an extra life to even things out. The battles were intense, including a super power your team gets with a one time use.

I worked on the front end, tweaking some loading of assets via soft pointers to keep the game optimised due to the very high fidelity.

I also worked on the export pipelines so we could cut down the amount of effort involved in importing new assets into the game. We used a lot of effects and these were done in a very efficient way using render targets and signed distance fields to avoid assets in some cases althogether!
