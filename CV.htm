About Me

I began programming at a young age on a ZX Spectrum using a book of BASIC listings for simple games. After joining secondary school, I spent lunch hours and time after school using QBASIC to develop my own games.

By the time I reached university I already knew C, but there I learnt C++ and in my spare time taught myself graphics programming.

Since leaving university, the experience I have gained has been varied. I have developed multi-threaded accounts systems, DTP applications, games, interfaces and tools for other game developers to use.
