About Me

I began programming at a young age on a ZX Spectrum using a book of BASIC listings for simple games after my dad encouraged me with second hand computers he picked up from electronics stores in the 1980's. After joining secondary school, I spent lunch hours and time after school using QBASIC to develop my own games.

My first project was a tool I used to hack Warcraft : Orcs And Humans (written using PDS which allowed building binaries from QBasic!)

By the time I reached university I already knew C, but there I learnt C++ and in my spare time taught myself graphics programming.

Since leaving university, the experience I have gained has been varied but mostly in the field of games. I have developed games for PS5, XBOX, Windows PC, Android and IOS. I have knowledge of shaders and export pipelines having written FBX importers and a number of API's for a number of developers in my roles across studios such as Epic, Wargaming, Play By Play and Zenimax.
