This was a project I saw from a prototype working in a very small indie team which had no artist support to start with. I lead development on the UI & UX making this work across all platforms and input devices. I worked on the animation on screens and the interactivity with a pragma backend. Coached another junior developer through the process to help me and managed a few other developers on tasks to assist development along the way. Localised across 5 languages I worked on all the elements in the above screenshots. These ranged from the in game HUD, to the unlocks shop and video settings, to the intro sequences and matchmaking screens which randomize court and rules. I also developed all the UI for all game modes and inviting players to join your squads.
